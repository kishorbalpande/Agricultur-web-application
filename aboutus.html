About Us

SublimityAgro is a Fresh Fruit packaging and exporting firm with its office based in Pandhurna,Madhyapradesh,India.We have a vast distribution network with strong logistics support,establishing a nationwide reach.SublimityAgro is the 2th generation in the fruit business since the year 2000 starting as a local fruit trading firm Kisan Santra Packing center,exporting fruits from year 2000 and further starting our own exporting from the year 20019 under the name SublimityAgro pvt ltd,thereon working with various fruit traders in India and abroad.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred Indian fruit trading business.

Our vision calls us to earn trust and create value for our key stakeholders:

  • Growers: By enabling their fruits reach from their farms to different regions in India
  • Wholesalers: By ensuring they receive fruits fresh, every day, every time
  • Consumers: By satiating their taste preferences through a gamut of fruit offerings
